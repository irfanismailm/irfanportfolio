  • Client: Daru Nashrati Taiba, India

BESTSELLER, More than 10,000 copies sold internationally

Mawlid Al-Husnain is a 334 pages Arabic book project with high complexity texts and designs. It was a double colour work and each page was edited many times sitting with the author. It took one month to complete the whole project including its cover and all other elements. I had continues discussion with the auther and printers a lot since any minor decision had majour impact on the cost of print. It was the second edition, and now Daru Nashratu Taiba is heading towards 6th edition with same design.
